Research Interests

My main research interests are in functional programming languages, type theory, compilers, embedded domain-specific languages, program verification and language semantics. The projects that I worked on had a focus on using sophisticated type systems to ease the development and formal verification of correct yet efficient operating systems components. I am also interested in logic, metamathematics, proof theory and formal methods in general.
